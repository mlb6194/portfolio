02 / Pharma Regulatory

Pharma Regulatory Guidance Agent

RAG · Document grounding · Compliance benchmarking

Live Demo

A RAG system grounded in ~60 ICH, FDA, EU GMP, and EMA regulatory documents, built for scientists and regulatory affairs teams who need precise, auditable answers from a large document corpus. Every response is evaluated with a calibrated confidence score, grounded in direct quotes with exact section citations, and constrained to the source corpus to prevent hallucination — designed for environments where accuracy is non-negotiable and every claim must be traceable.

Output Evaluation & Confidence Scoring
Every response includes a calibrated confidence score based on retrieval quality and source coverage
Automated self-evaluation layer that flags low-confidence answers before they reach the user
Confidence thresholds tuneable per use case — strict for regulatory submissions, flexible for exploratory queries
Citation Extraction & Source Grounding
Direct quotes extracted from source regulatory documents and surfaced inline with each answer
Exact section and paragraph references linked to ICH, FDA, EU GMP, and EMA guidance documents
Full audit trail — every claim is traceable back to a specific passage in the source corpus
Hallucination Prevention by Design
Responses grounded exclusively in the retrieved regulatory document corpus — no unverified claims
Retrieval-first architecture ensures the LLM reasons over real documents, not parametric memory
Answers that cannot be supported by the source material are explicitly flagged rather than fabricated

Architecture

60+ Regulatory Docs → ChromaDB Vectorstore → RAG Retrieval → Claude LLM → Cited Answers + Scores

Technical Highlights

Evaluation framework: Confidence scoring calibrated against retrieval precision and document coverage, giving users a quantified reliability signal on every response
Guardrails & safety: Retrieval-gated generation — the LLM cannot produce answers without supporting source material, eliminating fabricated regulatory guidance
Audit compliance: Full citation chain from user query → retrieved chunks → source document section, meeting traceability requirements for regulated industries
